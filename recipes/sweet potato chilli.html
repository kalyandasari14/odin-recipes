Sweet Potato Chilli

A feel-good Sweet Potato Black Bean Chili recipe that's satisfying and easy to make. It's full of rich, warm flavors and everyone can garnish their bowl with their own favorite toppings! (Vegan, gluten-free, oil-free.)


    Ingredients

  • 5 Tablespoons vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup Chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper,minced
  • 2 sweet potatoes,diced
  • 2 tablespoons chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable brooth
  • 1(28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups drained and rinsed kidney beans
    •

    Steps

  1. Heat 5 tablespoons vegetable broth in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Cook and stir onion,garlic,and jalapeno pepper in the hot broth until slightky tender,about 5 minutes
  3. Add sweet potatoes,chilli powder,cumin,paprika,oregano,and red pepper onion flakes to onion mixture.
  4. Pour remaining 2 cups vegetable broth over sweet potato mixture.
  5. Bring vegetable broth mixture to a boil,reduce heat to medium,and simmer until sweet potatoes are tender,20 t0 25 minutes
  6. stir tomatoes and kidney beans into sweet potato mixture and simmer over low heat until flavours have blended,about 45 minuutes
